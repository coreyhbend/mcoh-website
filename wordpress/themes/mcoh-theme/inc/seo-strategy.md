# SEO and Entity Strategy

## Primary Goal

Build Make Central Oregon Home into the most useful Central Oregon lifestyle and relocation resource online.

The site should become a trusted topical authority around:

- Central Oregon lifestyle
- Bend Oregon tourism
- Central Oregon relocation
- Outdoor recreation
- Local intel and events
- Neighborhood and community guides
- Restaurants, breweries, and local favorites

## SEO Architecture

Primary authority clusters:

1. Central Oregon Lifestyle
2. Bend Tourism and Things To Do
3. Outdoor Recreation
4. Destinations and Day Trips
5. Community and Neighborhood Guides

Each cluster should contain:

- One pillar page
- Multiple supporting pages
- Strong internal linking
- Related content sections
- Shared entities and locations

## Schema Priorities

Recommended schema types:

- Article
- Event
- Place
- LocalBusiness
- FAQPage
- BreadcrumbList
- ItemList
- Organization
- WebSite

## Homepage Entity Focus

Homepage should reinforce:

- Bend Oregon
- Central Oregon
- Smith Rock
- Mt Bachelor
- Cascade Lakes
- Sisters Oregon
- Redmond Oregon
- Sunriver
- High Desert Museum

## Internal Linking Strategy

Every guide should link to:

- Related communities
- Related destinations
- Related activities
- Related seasonal content
- Related local intel

## Content Velocity Goal

Initial target:

- 2 to 3 high quality guides per week
- 3 to 5 local intel updates weekly
- 1 weekly events roundup
- 1 seasonal or relocation article weekly

## Local Intel Strategy

The Local Intel system is one of the biggest long term differentiators.

Intel should include:

- Weather
- Smoke conditions
- Trail conditions
- Concerts
- Events
- Restaurant openings
- Brewery releases
- Development updates
- Community happenings
- Seasonal changes

## Long Term SEO Goal

The objective is not simply rankings.

The objective is:

- topical authority
- recurring direct traffic
- branded search growth
- LLM visibility
- destination authority
- local trust
- newsletter growth
- content flywheel momentum

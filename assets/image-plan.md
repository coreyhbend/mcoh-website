# Image Direction Plan

## Goal

Replace generic placeholder imagery with highly targeted Central Oregon visuals tied directly to communities, destinations, lifestyle themes, and editorial categories.

## Hero Images

Recommended subjects:

- Bend skyline at sunset
- Deschutes River through Bend
- Sparks Lake sunrise
- Smith Rock sunrise
- Mt Bachelor winter lifestyle
- Cascade Lakes Highway

## Community Images

### Northwest Crossing
- Walkable streets
- Local cafes
- Parks and trails
- Modern Bend lifestyle

### Midtown Bend
- Local restaurants
- Vintage Bend feel
- Central location
- Small businesses

### Sisters
- Western downtown
- Mountain views
- Outdoor lifestyle

### Redmond
- Canyon views
- Growth and development
- Outdoor recreation

## Destination Images

- Smith Rock
- Tumalo Falls
- Sparks Lake
- Mt Bachelor
- Old Mill District
- High Desert Museum
- Cascade Lakes Highway

## Image Folder Structure

```text
assets/images/

```

## Long Term Recommendation

Over time prioritize:

1. Original photography
2. Drone photography
3. Seasonal photo libraries
4. Hyperlocal neighborhood visuals
5. Editorial quality compositions
6. Video and motion assets

## Important Principle

Every major content section should visually reinforce:

- location
- lifestyle
- seasonality
- activity
- mood
- authenticity

Avoid generic stock photography whenever possible.

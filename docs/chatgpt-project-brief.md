## What This Project Is

We are building **makecentraloregonhome.com** — a premium Central Oregon lifestyle, tourism, relocation, and community resource platform. This is NOT a real estate website. It's a local publication, insider guide, and regional authority hub.

**The site should feel like:** a modern local publication meets tourism guide meets community resource — "Central Oregon insider knowledge from someone who genuinely lives here."

**Owner:** Corey Hanson — Central Oregon real estate agent and investor, Bend OR
**Real estate site (separate):** centralorproperties.com
**GitHub repo (private):** https://github.com/coreyhbend/mcoh-website

---

## Core Goal

Inspire people to visit, explore, and fall in love with Central Oregon — and eventually imagine themselves living there. Real estate exists quietly in the background as a natural next step. The site should never feel like a lead generation funnel.

Primary outcomes in order:
1. Become the most useful online guide to life in Central Oregon
2. Build SEO topical authority across tourism, lifestyle, relocation, and community content
3. Drive organic traffic from people researching Central Oregon
4. Build a locally trusted, highly shareable regional resource
5. Convert a small percentage of engaged readers into real estate conversations — naturally

---

## Brand Direction

**Colors:** Cobalt `#050E36`, Black, White, soft neutral grays. NO copper, gold, orange, or warm metallics.

**Typography:** Cormorant Garamond (serif headlines) + Inter (body). Spacious, minimal, image-forward.

**Tone:** Calm, knowledgeable, local, never salesy. Writes like a trusted local — not a travel blogger or a real estate agent. No hype language. No generic tourist copy.

**Visual direction:** Cinematic, editorial, outdoors-focused, premium but approachable, mountain lifestyle.

---

## Tech Stack

- **CMS:** WordPress (self-hosted)
- **Theme:** Custom PHP — built from HTML prototypes
- **SEO:** RankMath Pro
- **Feeds:** WP RSS Aggregator (news, events)
- **Performance:** WP Rocket + Cloudflare CDN
- **Images:** Cloudinary (CDN, WebP)
- **Events:** The Events Calendar plugin
- **Custom fields:** Advanced Custom Fields Pro
